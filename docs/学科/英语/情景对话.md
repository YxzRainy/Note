# 情景对话

## 打电话

1. hello! May I speak to xxx, please?
   1. This is xxx speaking.
   2. Speaking 
   3. Hold on,please
2. xxx died/xxx is ill 等不好的消息
   1. I‘am soory to hear that
3. 当对方夸奖你
   1. Thank you
   2. Thank you very much
4. 你犯错了
   1. It doesn't matter.
5. 对方祝你节日快乐
   1. The same to you.
6. 对方祝你生日快乐
   1. Thank you
7. 成功完成了一件事
   1. （Well done）Congratultions
8. 要去某个地方旅游
   1. have a good time
9. 某个物品多少钱
   1. here you are.
10. 祈使句命令
    1. sorry，I won't
11. would do you like...
    1. Sure, I'd love to/Sure, i am happy to.
12. How do you do
    1. How do you do
