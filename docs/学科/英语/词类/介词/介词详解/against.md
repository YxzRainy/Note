# against

## 表示接触

### against + 人或事物的词语

表示靠着某人某物。强调两个事物的直接接触。

- He sat against the tree.

- She stood against each other.

## 表示反对

### against + 某些词语

表示**反对...**。

- I am against this plan.

- Are you for this plan.
