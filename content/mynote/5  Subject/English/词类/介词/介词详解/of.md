# of

## 表示...的

### of + 无生命的名词所有格/动名词短语/代词/数词

表示**...的**

- The price of this coat.
- The color of my shirt.

### of + 比较长的生命的名词

表示.**...的**

- It is the story of my lady Mary, Queen of Scots.
- She is the daughter of my English teacher, Nick.
