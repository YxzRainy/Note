# beside

## 表示位置

### beside + 表示地点的名词

表示在这个某个地点的旁边或者附近。

- The room beside the bathroom is mine.

- She is beside the bathroom.
- My student drink tea beside the sea every afternoon.

## 表示对比

### beside + 名词或代词

表示与某人相比。

- Beside his, my trouble is nothing.

## 表示无关

- That is beside the question.
