like

表示像某个东西

like + 表示人或物的词语

表示像某个人或某个物一样。

  • The leaf is like a heart.
  • She is like her mother.
  • You should marry a man like me.

固定搭配

look like + 名词/代词宾格

表示看起来某个事物。

  • She looks like a singer.
  • The moom looks likes a silver plate.
  • It looks like rain today.
